TRANSAID CONSORTIUM DIRECTORS VISIT

TRANSAID CONSORTIUM DIRECTORS VISIT TO ITC ON 22/05/2024. In August 2008, Transaid partnered with the Industrial Training Centre Trust (ITC) the only public commercial driver training Centre in Zambia to build capacity to deliver professional HGV driver training to internationally recognized standards. The partnership with Transaid UK has elevated ITC's training capabilities in the transport sector. The donation of truck units, Forklift trucks and the computer simulator have enriched the learning experience for our students, while the training provided to our instructors has ensured that our programs remain at the forefront of industry standards. ITC is deeply grateful for Transaid UK's support, which has had a lasting and positive impact on both the institution and the future professionals trainees.
